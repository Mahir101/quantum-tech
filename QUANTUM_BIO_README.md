### 1. Quantum DNA Analytics (Q-Genomics)
Traditional DNA sequence alignment (like BLAST) is computationally expensive. We leverage quantum search to achieve quadratic speedups.
*   **Grover‚Äôs Pattern Matching:** Using Grover‚Äôs algorithm to locate specific genetic markers in unindexed genomic data.
*   **Quantum Walks on DNA:** Using Quantum Random Walks to model the diffusion of transcription factors across the double helix.
*   **De Novo Assembly (Quantum Annealing):** Formulating the DNA assembly problem as a QUBO (Quadratic Unconstrained Binary Optimization) problem for D-Wave systems.

### 2. Protein Origami (Quantum Folding)
While AlphaFold 2/3 uses Geometric Deep Learning, **Quantum Bio-Origami** uses the physics of the quantum state to simulate the "folding" energy landscape.
*   **VQE (Variational Quantum Eigensolver):** Calculating the ground-state energy of amino acid interactions to find the most stable "folded" state.
*   **Lattice Models (HP Model):** Mapping the Hydrophobic-Polar model of proteins onto a quantum chip where each qubit represents the "bend" or "twist" of the protein backbone.
*   **Quantum Geometric Deep Learning:** Integrating Quantum Neural Networks (QNNs) with E(3)-equivariant graphs to predict protein-ligand binding with sub-atomic precision.
